Sonic the Hedgehog

A small tribute page for my favorite videogame character

My story as a fan of sonic the hedgehog starts in the year 2000 when my uncle let me play sonic the hedgehog 3 on his pc. I was only 5 years old back then but i got hooked to that game's overall feel inmediately. I continued playing all sonic games a could get my hands into and haven't stopped to this day. So to thank this franchize for all the years of entertainment i am making a list of some of my favorite sonic games in no particular order:

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 2

    Sonic the Hedgehog 2 is a side-scrolling platform game. It features a story similar to the first Sonic the Hedgehog's (1991): Doctor Robotnik, a mad scientist, seeks the Chaos Emeralds to power his space station, the Death Egg, and traps the animal inhabitants of West Island in aggressive robots. More info.

  • Sonic the Hedgehog 3

    Sonic the Hedgehog 3 is a 2D side-scrolling platformer. As with Sonic the Hedgehog 2, players can control Sonic, Tails, or both simultaneously. In the former choice, players control Sonic while Tails runs along beside him; a second player can join at any time and control Tails separately. More info.

  • Sonic Heroes

    Sonic Heroes is a 3D platformer. While the preceding Sonic Adventure games for the Dreamcast featured elements of action-adventure and exploration, Sonic Heroes focuses on linear platforming and action. More info.

  • Sonic Generations

    Sonic Generations is a platforming video game, with bits of gameplay taken from past Sonic games. The player takes control of either Classic Sonic or Modern Sonic, each with their own abilities and levels. More info.

  • Sonic Frontiers

    As Sonic, the player explores the Starfall Islands (which consist of various biomes including flowery fields, forests, ancient ruins, and deserts) to collect the Chaos Emeralds and investigate the islands' relationship to them. More info.
